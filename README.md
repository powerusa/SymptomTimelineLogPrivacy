# Privacy Policy for Symptom Timeline Log

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Symptom Timeline Log is designed as a private, local-first health tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

## Information You Enter

Symptom Timeline Log lets you record symptoms, health events, medications, notes, tags, photos, reminders, and reports. This information is stored locally on your device.

The app does not require an account and does not upload your health entries to a server controlled by us.

## Data Collection

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health information.

Symptom Timeline Log does not include third-party advertising, third-party analytics, or third-party tracking SDKs.

## Photos and Attachments

If you add photos or attachments to an entry, those files are stored locally on your device as part of your app data.

## Reports and Exports

The app may let you create PDF, CSV, JSON, or other exports. Exports are created only when you choose to create or share them. You control where exported files are sent or saved.

## Reminders and Notifications

If you create reminders, the app may schedule local notifications on your device. Notification content is generated from information you enter in the app.

## Support Email

If you contact support, your email address and the information you include in your message will be received by us so we can respond to your request.

Support email: powersatellite@yahoo.com

## Children

Symptom Timeline Log is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Medical Disclaimer

Symptom Timeline Log is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, prediction, prevention, or recommendations. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
